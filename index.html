What's all this about then?

Look, we're all busy people. And we all face decisions every day. So why waste your own brain power trying to make those decisions, when a random answer will likely do just as well?

That's why I created Randomixr.

  • Pizza or burgers? Just flip a coin, and you're done.
  • Need to figure out which of the dozen options to try? Pick a random number and see how it goes.
  • Nobody wants to go on the beer run? Draw straws, and there's no more controversy.
  • Want to play a kissy game, but don't have a bottle? Try spinning a spinner! Also good for picking which direction to go next!

If you love this app and find it useful, why not consider chipping in a little bit of beer money for me?
